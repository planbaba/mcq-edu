questions:
  - question: What is 1/2 of 10?
    choices:
      - 2
      - 5
      - 8
      - 10
    answer: 5
    explanation: Half means two equal groups. Ten split into two equal groups gives five in each group.
    fun_fact: Pizza slices are tasty fraction models when everyone gets an equal share.
  - question: Which fraction shows one out of four equal parts?
    choices:
      - 1/2
      - 1/3
      - 1/4
      - 3/4
    answer: 1/4
    explanation: The bottom number tells how many equal parts there are. Four equal parts makes quarters.
    fun_fact: A quarter coin is called a quarter because it is one fourth of a dollar.
