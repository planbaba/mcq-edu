## What is a fraction?

A fraction tells us how many equal parts we have. The **top number** is the numerator. The **bottom number** is the denominator.

> Comic Callout: If the denominator is 4, the whole is split into 4 equal pieces!

### Quick diagram

`1/2` means one of two equal parts.  
`1/4` means one of four equal parts.

### Learning summary

- Fractions must use equal parts.
- The denominator counts total equal parts.
- The numerator counts selected parts.
